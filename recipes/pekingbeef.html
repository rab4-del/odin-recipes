Peking-Style Beef

Description

Cooks fasts and requires zero effort - no chopping, no marinating, no stress! Sweet-savoury magic but weeknight-friendly in minced ground beef

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 3 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of brown sugar

Beef

  • 1 tablespoon light olive oil
  • 500g regular minced beef
  • 1 teaspoon freshly minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon freshly grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon cornflour mixed with 2 tablespoon of water

To serve

  • Steamed jasmine rice
  • 2 cucumbers, peeled into ribons
  • 2 spring onions, thinly sliced
  • Toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 seaspoon sesame oil

Steps

  1. In a jug or small bowl, combine sauce ingredients, set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a large heavy-based pan over high heat. Add beef, break it up, and allow it to cook, untouched for at least 5-6 minutes
  3. Flip it, further breaking it apart with your spatula and cook for a further 1-2 minutes or until cooked through
  4. Stir the garlic and ginger in, cooking for 30 minutes
  5. Pour the sauce over the beef and simmer for 1-2 minutes to coat the beef.
  6. Stir in the cornflour and water mixture. Cook for 30-60 seconds until the sauce thickens and clings to the beef
  7. Spoon over rice and top with cucumber ribbons, spring onion, sesame seeds and a drizzle of sesame oil
