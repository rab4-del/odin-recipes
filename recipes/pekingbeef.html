Peking-Style Beef

Description

Cooks fasts and requires zero effort - no chopping, no marinating, no stress! Sweet-savoury majic but weeknight-friendly in minced ground beef

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 3 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of brown sugar

Beef

  • 1 tablespoon light olive oil
  • 500g regular minced beef
  • 1 teaspoon freshly minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon freshly grated ginger
  • 1 teaspoon cornflour mixed with 2 tablespoon of water

To serve

  • Steamed jasmine rice
  • 2 cucumbers, peeled into ribons
  • 2 spring onions, thinly sliced
  • Toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 seaspoon sesame oil
