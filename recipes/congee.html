Congee

Description

Also known as jook, this traditional savory Chinese breakfast porridge is quick and easy, made using chicken thighs, rice, corn and ginger. It is topped with sliced spring onions, sesame seeds and chilli sauce. Great flavor and flexible.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup uncooked short-grain white rice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 14 ounces boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 piece grate fresh ginger
  • 1 ear fresh corn, husked
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 spring onions, sliced
  • 6 teaspoons black sesame seeds
  • 6 teaspoons spicy chili crisp sauce
